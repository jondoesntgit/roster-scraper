Helpful Information

This page allows you to view room assignments for a building. For this building, all assignments whose begin dates or end dates fall between the begin and end dates you selected will be displayed. Also, if you select an assignment status, only those assignments having the selected status are displayed.

Sorting:

- You may sort a list of assignment by any heading in ascending or descending order.

- When the list is first displayed, the assignments are sorted by student name in ascending order.
